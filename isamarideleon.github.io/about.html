Profile

Isa Mari De Leon (or Isa for short) is a student of various disciplines. They currently pursue a BA in English from the University of California, Berkeley. Prior to attending Cal, Isa went to Chabot College in Hayward, California. There, they studied English and liberal arts.

With a diverse background in writing, education, publishing, and game development, Isa's passions center around storytelling and collaboration. Isa is currently based in the San Francisco Bay Area. For business inquiries, email isamarideleon@gmail.com.
